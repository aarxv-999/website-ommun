About This Committee

“Strong health, stronger world.”

World Health Assembly is the highest authority in health-based policymaking, setting the agenda for global cooperation on medicine and healthcare. At OM MUN 2025, the Junior Committee allows younger participants to step into this world, debating issues like disease prevention, universal healthcare, and medical equity while gaining first-hand insight into global health governance.
