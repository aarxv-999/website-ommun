Letter from the Conference President

It is with great pleasure and anticipation, I extend a formal invitation to the OM MUN 2025, scheduled to take place on the 24th, 25th, and 26th October 2025 at Birla Open Minds International School, Kollur.

This year OM-MUN ventures into its 1st Inter School Conference. We are thrilled to open our doors to schools from across the region, creating a platform for young minds to come together, engage in rigorous debate, and explore solutions to the world's most pressing issues.

Our commitment to the quality of debate is at the heart of our conference. We believe that true learning happens when delegates are encouraged to think critically, challenge ideas, and develop innovative solutions.

FAUZIA SIDDIQUI

Conference President, OM MUN 2025
